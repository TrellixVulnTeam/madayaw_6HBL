MinNa Laboratory Blessing and MOU Signing
By: Aurora Cristina A. Manseras
Posted on December 10, 2021




The blessing of the Mindanao Natural (MinNa) Language Processing (LProc) Research and Development Laboratory was held last December 8, 2021 at the University of the Immaculate Conception (UIC), Fr. Selga campus.

The event started with a mass at 8:30 AM celebrating the Solemnity of the Immaculate Conception of the Blessed Virgin Mary. This was officiated by Rev. Fr. Rene Ribac. The blessing took place afterwards and was attended by guests and several members of the UIC Community. Also present during this occasion were Engr. Howell A. Ong, R&D for Development and Innovation Division of the Davao Region Industry, Energy, and Emerging Technologies Research and Development Consortium (DRIEERDC), Department of Science and Technology (DOST) Region XI, and Mr. Rod John Pandili, Founder and President of the Lumon Community Association.

A Memorandum of Understanding was also signed between MinNa LProc and the Lumon Community Association, a non-governmental organization providing charitable services to transform communities at the grassroots level of society through active volunteerism. The MOU is an agreement between MinNa LProc and the Lumon Community to work together as collaborating partners in the conduct of the activities and projects of the laboratory.
